Changing the way gamers play

SteamLFG is filled with amazing features to enhance your gaming experience and set you on the right track towards a healthier lifestyle. Here is some of what SteamLFG has to offer:

Create Groups

SteamLFG allows you the ability to get your friends together and make a group. The group feature will allow you to find new games that work for you and your friends, and will also give you the ability to checck your time spent playing together.

 Find Groups

What if you dont't have a group? That's perfectly fine, as SteamLFG allows you to browse groups for games that you play, and you'll find the right people to help you with your gaming journey!

 User Profile

Everyone loves to be unqiue, and SteamLFG gives each user the ablity to express who they are. The ability to edit your profile how you like it will allow visiitng users to see what you really are about.

 Time Monitor

SteamLFG will tackle down on vidoe game addiction through goal-setting, daily monitoring of hours played, and regular suggestions to unlock a better lifestyle in relation to video games and gaming. SteamLFG will also monitor a user's time played, and will incentivize healthy habits through achievements and increases in profile level.
